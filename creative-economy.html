Policy 03

Make room for events, arts, and local jobs

Anchorage can make local events, music, markets, filming, public spaces, and business corridors easier to use, easier to find, and harder to ignore.

The mission

Creative work becomes local economic development when city government clears practical barriers: permits, public space access, vendor rules, safety coordination, and partnerships that connect young people to real work.

Simpler local permitting

Make event, market, and small-production permits clearer, faster, and easier to navigate for local organizers and small businesses.

Workforce pathways

Partner with ASD, UAA, trades, and creative professionals on apprenticeships, internships, and technical training for production, events, and venue work.

Events that lift corridors

Use festivals, music, markets, and cultural events to bring foot traffic to District 5 businesses and make public spaces feel active and welcoming.

Tell Anchorage stories locally

Support projects that highlight Indigenous, local, and neighborhood stories with respect, collaboration, and real community benefit.
