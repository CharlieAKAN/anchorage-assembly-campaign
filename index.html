Hills To Stand On

Grounded values. No fog machine.

Education is the long game

Support youth, teachers, libraries, and career pathways that prepare residents for a changing economy.

Protect personal freedom

Defend pro-choice and LGBTQ+ policies, and keep local government welcoming and fair.

Respect subsistence and culture

Honor Alaska Native communities, local knowledge, and the ways families sustain themselves.

Make essentials affordable

Focus on housing, utilities, transportation, child care, and the everyday costs families actually feel.

Build for clean energy

Back renewables, efficiency, and practical innovations that lower long-term costs.

Invest in small businesses

Make permitting clearer, events easier to host, and city processes less painful for local employers.
